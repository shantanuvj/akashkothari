Our Services

Investment and Wealth Management

Mutual Funds

Mutual Funds are investment instruments that combine different instruments such as stocks or shares, bonds or both into a single product which is managed by an expert fund manager. This is like pooling your money with many other investors to buy a diversified basket of stocks, bonds, or other assets making it an easy way for individuals to invest in the market.

Portfolio Management Services (PMS)

PMS is a tailor-made service where a professional manager actively invests your capital in a personalized portfolio of stocks, fixed income, or other securities. It is for individuals who want a highly customized and hands-on approach to managing their wealth.

Specialised Investment Funds (SIF)

SIF is a new asset class that aims to bridge the gap between MFs and PMS/AIFs, offering a blend of flexibility and unique investment strategies. SIFs offer more flexibility to investment managers as compared to traditional money managers, while still providing a level of regulatory oversight and investor protection.

Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)

AIFs are unique investment vehicles that are privately pooled and invested in alternative asset classes such as venture capital, private equity, hedge funds, commodities, real estate and derivatives. They are typically aimed at sophisticated investors seeking higher potential returns and diversification beyond traditional markets.

Insurance and Protection

Health Insurance

This product helps cover medical expenses like hospitalization, doctor visits, and surgeries, protecting you from high costs if you get sick or have an accident. By paying a regular premium, you secure peace of mind knowing your health care is financially supported.

Term Insurance

Term insurance provides financial protection for your family for a specific period of time (the "term"). If the insured person passes away during this term, the family receives a lump sum; if they live past the term, the policy simply ends with no payout.

Accidental Insurance

This coverage specifically provides a payout or reimbursement if you suffer an injury, disability, or death as a direct result of an accident. It acts as an important financial safety net, supplementing your health or life insurance.
